Features

The Bridge iOS Application gives users the power of the Bridge Platform on the go.

Refer a Client

Select professionals from your network and quickly send them in an email to clients you cannot currently take in.

Connect with other Professionals

See who you know on our platform and connect with them. Connect with colleagues in the area, from your school, or anyone in your professional network.

Showstopping Profiles

View and make changes to your profile. Update your picture or availability on the go. Also view your connections and colleagues profiles.

More coming soon

There is more where this came from. We are always working on adding new features to enhance your Bridge experience.

Installation

The app is deployed through Apple's TestFlight. This gives a select group of users access to download and test the application before giving it to the public.
